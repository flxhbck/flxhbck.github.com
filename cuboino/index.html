about cuboino

cuboino is a tangible, digital extension for the marble-game cuboro.

It consists of a set of cubes that are seamlessly compatible with the cuboro cubes. In contrast to the passive cuboro cubes, cuboino modules are active parts of a digital system consisting of sensor cubes, actor cubes and supply cubes. By snapping them together, the playercan build a modular system that functions according to he individual functionalities of the cuboino cubes.

cuboino establishes a new pathway that is not embodied in the marble, but adapts to the medium of its transmission. Signals can be received by multiple modules, creating more than one signal at a time. This allows signals to intertwine and thus create more dynamic and complex outcomes.


For more informations have a look at my bachelor-thesis.

cuboino cubes

light actor cube

When activated, this cube sheds a light into the direction it is pointed at. The cube can be used to activate the light-sensor cube or just for debugging of a track.

sound actor cube

This cube emits a sound of a pre-defined frequency when activated. It can be used to activate the light-sensor cube or just for debugging purposes.

shunting switch actor cube

This cube acts like a shunting switch for the marble track. When activated, the shunting turns 90 degrees and thus leads the marble into the opposite direction.

release actor cube

This cube can hold a bunch of marbles. When activated, the cube releases one single marble onto the track. This makes this cube the ideal starting point for a marble track.

light sensor cube

When the ambient light level rises quickly, this cube is activated. This can be caused by shedding a light onto the sensor side of the cube or quickly removing a shadow and thus increasing the amount of light. Hence this cube detects an activated light-actor cube if aligned correctly.

sound sensor cube

This cube is able to sense sound of a pre-defined frequency. It also reacts to loud noises when occuring in the right frequency.

sound sensor cube

When a marble runs through the track on top of the cube, a light barrier is intersected and the cube is activated. The cube can also be activated by putting anything else into the light-barrier.

button sensor cube

This cube offers the player an opportunity to directly interact with built systems. When the button on the side is pushed, the cube is activated. Additionally, this cube makes it easy to test functions of action cubes, as the system needed to see an action is minimal.

energy supply cube

This cube has to be part of every working cuboino network as it supplies energy to all cubes physically connected. Multiple energy cubes are allowed in one network.

connection supply cube

This cube just transmits energy and signals and multiplexes the outputs with its six connectable sides.

cuboino networks

Due to its modular nature, cuboino cubes allow for different systems to be assembled by the player. Here a selection of systems and concepts are presented in order to showcase the possibilities of cuboino cubes.
