Idea

In order to analyze human relations with objects, we researched within our team the different perceptions of common sports, popular sports, our reactions towards new sports and came up with concepts of our own in order to categorize all of them. Regular sports, modified sports (hacked) and new categories, like the controversial sport were introduced and taken into account.

BaseCross

BaseCross, combining the good parts of baseball and lacrosse, allows for a batting sport with a lacrosse-like team trying to score on a goal. Basically it allows the goalkeeper to carry a baseball bat and introduce quick fastbreaks that change the game's momentum. The result is the fun of baseball combined with a ongoing lacrosse game.

Controversial sports

The book contains self constructed concepts for controversial sports, some rather unlikely to ever be executed, such as the Piggyback ride polo, which will have two teams compete, the one from the richer country on the backs of the other one. Another idea suggests sprinklers in tennis courts for women with white clothing. But the truth is: There is a maximum length for womens pants in beachvolleyball.
